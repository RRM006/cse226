# BSCSE Program Requirements
## North South University – Department of Electrical & Computer Engineering
## School of Engineering & Physical Sciences (SEPS)

---

# Program Metadata

- Degree: Bachelor of Science in Computer Science and Engineering (BSc CSE)
- Total Credits Required: 130
- Minimum CGPA: 2.00
- Duration: 4 Years (8 Semesters)
- Waivable Courses: ENG102, MAT116
- Retake Policy: Best grade counts
- Maximum Attempts per Course: 3

# GRADUATION REQUIREMENTS

1. Total Credits ≥ 130  
2. CGPA ≥ 2.00  
3. All University Core Completed  
4. All SEPS Core Completed  
5. All Major Core Completed  
6. Capstone Completed  
7. Trail Requirement Completed  
8. Open Elective Completed  
9. No Probation Status  

---

# PROBATION RULE

- CGPA < 2.00 → Probation  
- Cannot graduate while on probation  

---

# RETAKE POLICY

- Best grade counts  
- Maximum 3 attempts  
- 4th attempt requires approval
