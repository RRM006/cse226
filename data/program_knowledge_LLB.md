# LL.B Honors Program Requirements

## Program Metadata
- **Program Name:** Bachelor of Laws (LL.B Honors)
- **Degree:** LL.B Honors
- **Total Credits Required:** 130
- **Waivable Courses:** ENG102
- **Department:** School of Law
